K.S.R. COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

ABOUT THE COLLEGE:

K.S.R. College of Engineering, Tiruchengode, is an autonomous institution affiliated with Anna University, Chennai. Established in 2001 under the Aarthi Educational and Charitable Trust by our revered Founder and late Chairman, Dr. K. S. Rangasamy MJF, the college has steadily grown in academic excellence. Currently, Thiru R. Srinivasan, the younger son of our founder, serves as the Chairman and Managing Trustee of the institution. The college offers 13 undergraduate (B.E. & B.Tech.) and 10 postgraduates (M.E. & M.Tech.) programmes, along with M.C.A. and MBA courses. In addition, KSRCE offers 8 Ph.D. programmes in Engineering, Science, and Computer Applications, all approved by AICTE, New Delhi. The institution is accredited by NAAC with an A++ grade, and eligible programmes are accredited by NBA, reflecting its commitment to quality and continuous improvement in technical education
